Levels

I created the LEVELs classification technique to categorize market price levels. It is based on Keltner Channels. The bands I use are comprised of two 14 day ATRs multiplied by 3 and placed above and below an 80 day exponential moving average. I found these settings to work well for market averages The levels are analogous to notes on a music scale with LEVEL_B as the middle line on the Treble Clef
